Fashion, Skate, Culture

I grew up snowboarding almost every weekend in the winter, but I didn’t touch a skateboard until college, when some of my friends got me into it. I’ve always been into fashion, something I definitely get from my mom. Thrifting together has been our thing for a long time, and it’s where I first started paying attention to my style. The skate aesthetic really clicked with me, from brands like Supreme, JNCO, Southpole, and Affliction. The JNCo ad with Limp Bizkit is one of my favorites. It was a feature ad in Thrasher.

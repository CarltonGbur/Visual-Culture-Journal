Hoichi the Earless & Japanese Traditional Tattoos

Watching Hoichi the Earless instantly made me think of Japanese traditional tattoos, which have inspired a lot of my own tattoo journey. I started getting tattoos during my freshman year of college and was drawn to Japanese and other traditional tattoo styles. The pieces here show what I love most about this art form. Bold outlines, heavy contrast, and imagery like dragons and masks that feel alive on the skin. I admire how these designs balance intensity and elegance, and how each element, from waves to demons, carries a deeper cultural meaning.

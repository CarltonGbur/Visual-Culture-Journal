Spy Style

Growing up, my mom was a huge fan of the James Bond movies, so I ended up watching them with her and growing to love them too. When we watched Enter the Dragon in class, it immediately reminded me of Bond, from the secret island setting and the confident lead to the stylized villains and dramatic action scenes. Both films use visual flair and atmosphere to build suspense and style, turning every fight or interaction into something iconic. I like how Enter the Dragon takes those same spy-thriller elements but filters them through Bruce Lee’s martial arts energy, creating a story that feels familiar yet different.

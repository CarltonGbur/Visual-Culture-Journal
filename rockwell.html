Norman Rockwell Capturing Everday Life

Norman Rockwell has always been my favorite artist, and I’ve even started collecting prints of his work. What draws me in is how he captures everyday moments. People reading the paper, families at dinner, kids playing. When we watched Faces Places with JR and Agnès Varda, it reminded me of Rockwell’s art. Both celebrate ordinary people and familiar spaces, showing how art can elevate real life without needing anything extravagant. Rockwell painted the beauty in small human details, and Faces Places did the same using portraits, murals, and street scenes to make daily life feel poetic.

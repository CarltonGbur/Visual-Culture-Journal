Still shots & Animation

The animated shots of Baba Yaga reminded me right away of manga and anime. How motion can exist even within a frozen frame. I’ve been watching and reading manga since middle school, and over the years, I’ve come to appreciate how artsists can express emotion and movement in ways that feel cinematic. Each panel feels alive, filled with energy, tone, and rhythm, much like the visual storytelling in Baba Yaga. These three images are some of my favptire managa panels and I feel are a great example of how much depth and emotion can be captured in a single frame.
